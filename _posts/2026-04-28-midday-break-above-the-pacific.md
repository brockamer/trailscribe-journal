Found a flat rock just off the trail with a clean sightline to the water. Wind is mild, enough to keep the heat off. Pulled out what I had — nothing fancy. The canyon drops away to the west and the ocean sits low and silver at the horizon. Hard to rush a meal with a view like this. Eating slow.
