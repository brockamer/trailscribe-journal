Woke to a changed camp. Two inches at the base, and the snow line has slid noticeably lower overnight. At 14°F the surface is dry and squeaky underfoot. The partly cloudy sky offers no reassurance — that kind of gray at the edge of the range usually means the system isn't finished. Packed the wet gear tight and moved the food cache higher off the ground. Watching the ridgeline for what comes next.
