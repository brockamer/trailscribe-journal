Salt wind off the bluffs
news still warm inside my chest
clouds part, then close back

Walked out to the edge of the bluff after the call ended. 53 degrees and the ocean doing its usual indifferent thing — swells rolling in, kelp smell, a few pelicans low over the water. Didn't feel like going back to the car right away. Stood there long enough for the cold to work through my jacket. The good feeling stays, even as the clouds close back in. Malibu doesn't care either way, which is somehow exactly right.
