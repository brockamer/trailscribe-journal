The headache hit around 13k, that familiar band tightening across the forehead. Stopped to down another half-liter and shook two ibuprofen into my palm. Wind was cutting, maybe twelve miles out of the northwest, cold enough to sting exposed skin. Partly cloudy kept the sun from adding much warmth. I know the drill up here in the Inyos — slow the pace, stay ahead of the dehydration, don't push ego over elevation. Watching my own signals carefully.
