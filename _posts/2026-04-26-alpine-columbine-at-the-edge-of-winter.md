Stumbled on it just off the ridge line — a columbine tucked into a south-facing crack where the wind drops enough to let something delicate survive. At 12°F it looked improbable, almost defiant. Left flagging tape on the nearest cairn. The coordinates are logged. This one warrants a proper return when the light is better and the ground isn't iron-hard. Inyo has a way of hiding things until you're almost past them.
