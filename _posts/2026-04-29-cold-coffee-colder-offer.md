Bitter cup goes cold
words dissolve like instant grounds
the canyon waits still

Sat on the tailgate with a tin mug of instant, watching the ridgeline go gold. The recruiter call came in right on schedule — polished voice, stock phrases, the usual pitch. Said my name twice like that would help. I let the silence after their question run a beat too long. The chaparral didn't care either way. Hung up, finished the coffee cold, and watched a hawk work the thermals. The canyon had a better offer.
