Made the pass. Took longer than it should have — talus navigation by headlamp is slow work, each block tilting just enough to keep you honest. Fourteen degrees and a steady wind off the crest. The clouds broke enough to show the ridge line, which helped. Legs are heavy. Glad to be here, gladder still to stop moving uphill. Bishop Pass doesn't give much away in the dark, but it's solid ground and that's enough tonight.
