cold summit at dawn

blue holds everything

Topped out at 0612. Twenty-eight degrees and dead calm — rare enough up here that it felt almost suspicious. Sky a hard, clean blue from horizon to horizon. The granite was dusted but not buried. Legs felt the miles but the stillness made it easy to just stand there a while. No one else on the summit block. Signed nothing, said nothing. Turned back before the wind found its way up.
