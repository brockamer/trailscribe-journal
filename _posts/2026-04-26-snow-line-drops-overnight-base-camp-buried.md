Woke to a white floor where bare dirt had been. Snow line dropped hard overnight — faster than the forecast suggested. Two inches at base camp by first light, and the peaks above the Inyos are socked in on the north faces. Fourteen degrees at first check, wind out of the northwest at a steady twelve. Gear is staged under the tarp but I'll need to reassess the route. If this keeps up, the upper approach will be a different problem entirely.
