Came back to find the bag torn open and a fat marmot face-deep in the couscous. Didn't even flinch. We stared at each other for a moment — he won. Temps dropping hard tonight, probably why he's desperate. Can't really blame him. Hung what's left of my food and made do with a bar and some tea. Classic Inyo. Classic marmot.
