Reached the closure boundary below Forester around midday. NPS rope strung across the trail, sign citing active rockfall on the north face. Hard to argue — I could hear intermittent cracking somewhere above the cornice line. Twelve degrees and a steady wind out of the northwest. Stood there a few minutes, watched a chunk of debris skitter down the chute. Turned back south. No shame in it.
