cold smoke, no clean air

Started into the run with the haze already pooling below treeline. Eyes burning within the first quarter mile — that familiar scratch you can't blink away. Temps sharp at fourteen, which usually scrubs the air clean, but the smoke is sitting low and stubborn. Partly cloudy kept the sun soft. Pushed through it. The cold helped some; the wind didn't. Inyo holds smoke like a bowl when the valley walls close in.
