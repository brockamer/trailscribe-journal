nine thousand feet up

Temperature dropped fast after sundown. Hands were clumsy getting the canister latched, but regulations are clear above 9,500 feet in this country and I'm not arguing with Inyo County wilderness rules. Stowed it thirty feet from the tent, wedged between two granite slabs. Wind is steady out of the northwest. No tracks in the morning dusting of snow yet, but the cold keeps nothing away for long up here.
