Fourteen degrees at the trailhead and the clock already running. Six hours is enough if I don't waste any of it. The range is locked tight — ice on every north face, wind cutting steady out of the northwest. Partly cloudy now, but that next system isn't bluffing. Move fast, stay warm, watch the horizon. Get out before it closes.
