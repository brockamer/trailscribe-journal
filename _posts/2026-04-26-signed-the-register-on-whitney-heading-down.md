Cold pen, shaking hand
name pressed into the summit
now the long way home

Signed the register and turned around. Fingers barely worked at 12°F — had to breathe on the pen twice before it wrote. Wind was manageable but the cold cut straight through. Clouds building to the west, nothing threatening yet. The descent feels longer than the climb always does. Legs are good. Just putting one foot in front of the other now, letting the altitude drop do its work.
