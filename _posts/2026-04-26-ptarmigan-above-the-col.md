white birds on white snow
three shapes the cold wind erases
still, then gone to sky

Crested the col and nearly stepped past them. Three ptarmigan, full winter plumage, standing motionless against the snowfield. At 14°F they seemed unbothered — just watched me with flat dark eyes before drifting apart into the rocks. Hard to say where bird ended and snowpack began. Wind steady out of the northwest. Legs tired from the climb but glad I paused long enough to count them.
