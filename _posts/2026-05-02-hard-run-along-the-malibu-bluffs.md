gray clouds press the hills
legs burn through the cold sea wind
the mind goes quiet

Pushed hard today. Wind off the Pacific had teeth, the kind that cuts through a light layer and reminds you the coast doesn't care about comfort. Sky stayed low and flat, that particular Malibu gray that turns the chaparral silver. Legs found a rhythm somewhere past the first mile and the noise in my head just stopped. Cold outside, something warmer moving inside. Came back clearer than I left.
