Cold stone shifts below
the narrow beam finds each step
pass opens at last

Fourteen degrees and the talus was slow going — every block a small negotiation in the dark. Kept the beam low, watched for shadow depth. Hands out twice for balance. The wind picked up near the saddle, thin and direct. When the pass finally opened up the sky was partly clear, stars sharp over the Palisades. Logged the split and kept moving. No time to linger at that temperature, but worth noting: the route holds.
