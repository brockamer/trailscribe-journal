Cold pen, shaking hand
name pressed into the cold page
down is the way now

Signed the register. Fingers barely worked at that temperature — had to press hard to get ink to take. Wind was manageable on the summit but the chill cut through everything. Partly cloudy kept the light flat. No long pause up there; the body knows when to move. Descending now, legs finding the rhythm back down the switchbacks. The hard part is done. The cold part is not.
