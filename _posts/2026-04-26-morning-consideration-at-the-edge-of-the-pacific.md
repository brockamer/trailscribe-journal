Fifty-three degrees and clear. The kind of morning that makes a beach run sound reasonable from inside, then humbling the moment you step out. Wind off the water is steady, nothing brutal, but enough to remind you the coast runs on its own schedule. Malibu stretches long and mostly empty this early. Could go. Probably will.
