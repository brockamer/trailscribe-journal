Left the device behind at the trailhead. Navigation by contour and instinct now — the way a slope bends tells you more than any screen. Followed a dry wash until the canyon opened. The land holds its own logic if you slow down enough to read it. No coordinates, no confirmation. Just the next ridge, and the one after that.
