The temperature had already dropped hard by the time the light changed. Fog sitting low on the water, diffusing the last color into something pale and diffuse — not the sharp alpenglow I'd hoped for, but quieter. The granite shoulders of the basin barely visible. Ice along the shallows. No wind to speak of. Just the cold pressing in and that muted orange dissolving into grey above the eastern Sierra crest.
