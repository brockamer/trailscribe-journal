cold light finds the peaks
rose and amber, then nothing
the lake holds it still

Woke before first light and posted up at the water's edge. Temperature already biting at 26, wind cutting across the surface. The alpenglow came fast — that brief flush of pink and orange across the granite before the sun cleared the ridge. The lake caught every bit of it. Partly cloudy kept the color honest, no overcast to flatten it. Gone in under two minutes. Worth the cold.
