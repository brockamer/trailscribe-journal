blue sky, nothing else

Topped out at 0612. Twenty-eight degrees and dead calm — rare for the crest. The light came flat and clean across the Alabama Hills far below, the kind of morning that makes the pre-dawn hurt worth it. Stayed long enough to eat, to look, to feel the stillness settle in. No one else on the summit. Descent started before the wind found its way back up the east face.
