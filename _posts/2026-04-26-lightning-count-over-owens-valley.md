cold flash offshore
counting Mississippi slow
thunder does not come

Nineteen miles in and the sky put on a show to the east — white forks dropping somewhere over the valley floor, far enough that I never heard the roll. Counted Mississippi after each flash out of habit. Nothing. Air at twelve degrees has a way of carrying light without sound, or maybe the storm was just keeping its distance. Kept moving. No reason to stop.
