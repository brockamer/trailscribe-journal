Bold thief in cold camp
whiskers dusted with couscous
no shame, only grain

Came back from the creek to find the bag torn open and a yellow-bellied marmot elbow-deep in dinner. Fourteen degrees and the little opportunist is still out here working angles. Didn't even startle when I got close — just chewed, regarded me, chewed again. Classic high-country shakedown. Repacked what was left, added a rock to the food pile. Lesson relearned at roughly 11,000 feet.
