no signal up here
the ridge decides where I go
map and gut agree

Packed the GPS away at the trailhead. Three days in and I don't miss it. The contour lines have been honest company — more so than any blue dot. Bearing by ridge, pace by feel. Made the col before dark using nothing but the topo and a hunch that proved right. There's a particular quiet that comes when you stop waiting for the device to confirm what your eyes already know.
