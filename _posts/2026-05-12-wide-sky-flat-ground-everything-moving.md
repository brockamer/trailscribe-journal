grassland blurs below
wind takes whatever I give
the sky holds it all

Heat off the hardpan, wind steady out of the southwest. The land here asks nothing of you — just flat miles and a horizon that keeps its distance. Moving fast through it feels less like travel and more like surrender. Something loosens. The afternoon light is sharp and honest and I am glad to be inside it.
