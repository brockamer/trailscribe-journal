Sitting with it this morning — the butter coffee, the 53-degree air, the light coming up slow over the water. The Pacific looks almost bronze at this hour. Wind off the ocean, maybe ten miles an hour, just enough to remind you it's real. Hard to feel anything but grateful in a place like this, with people like this. No agenda. Just the shore, the cup, the color of the sky.
