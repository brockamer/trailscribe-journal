Woke to 14°F and found the creek locked solid at the outlet. No open water anywhere along the near bank. Melted snow on the stove for the morning's water — slow work. The lake surface groaned once in the early light, probably thermal contraction. Wind out of the northwest, steady at 12, cutting across the basin without much to stop it. Partly cloudy but bright enough to hurt the eyes on the snow. Will check the far inlet before moving on.
