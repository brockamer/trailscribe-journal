Woke to 14°F and silence where the creek usually talks. The outlet at Charlotte Lake had gone solid overnight — no open water anywhere I could see from camp. Broke a hole near the bank with a heel strike but got only more ice. Will wait for the sun to work on the shallows before filtering. Wind out of the northwest, steady enough to cut through the shell. Partly cloudy but cold light coming through. Everything still.
