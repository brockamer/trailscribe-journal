Arms cut through cold air
blocks dissolve into the grey
sweat meets ocean mist

Set up the headset facing the window. Fog sitting low on the water, barely a horizon line out there. Didn't matter — once the music hit I was gone. Worked through three sets, arms burning by the second. The room stayed cold, 55 outside bleeding through the glass. Took the headset off after and just stood there, chest heaving, watching the grey. Malibu quiet except for the surf below.
